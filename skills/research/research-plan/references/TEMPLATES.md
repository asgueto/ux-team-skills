# Research Plan Templates

Copy and fill this in first. It anchors the plan in Condens and makes it findable later.

```

Product: [Product name]
Researcher(s): [Names]
PM: [Name]
Stakeholders: [Names, teams]
Designer: [Name, if applicable]
Method: [Qualitative interviews / Usability testing / Survey / etc.]
Status: [Planning / In progress / Finished]
Start date: [YYYY-MM-DD]
End date: [YYYY-MM-DD]

Related artifacts:
- [Link to previous research on this topic, if applicable]
- [Link to prototype/design, if applicable]
- [Link to product specs or PRD]
```

## When to Use Each Template

**Minimal plan:**
- Time-pressured (days, not weeks)
- Evaluative research (testing a specific hypothesis)
- Small participant set
- Clear, focused goal

**Full plan:**
- Exploratory or generative research
- Multi-method study
- Larger stakeholder group needing alignment
- Long timeline
- High-stakes decision
